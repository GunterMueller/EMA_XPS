EXPLANATION-FACILITY

Explanation Facility
====================

EMA-XPS does not offer the powerful explanation
facility of babylon3 (See "Unsupported").

Currently only an interactive graphic front end
for the outputs of the Babylon2 inference engine 
is availbale.

The LISP commands, generated by the menu entries
of the explanation window (see respective page),
may be used in KBs, too. When they are evaluated,
the explanation window appears and the interac-
tively requested explanations will be written
into it.
