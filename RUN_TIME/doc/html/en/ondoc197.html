OR

OR
==

syntax: (or {<form> ...})

This function evaluates each form, one at a time,
from left to right, until one form evaluates to
something other than NIL. In this case this value
will be returned, otherwise NIL.

example:       >(or nil t)
               T
               >(or nil nil)
               NIL
