AND
===

syntax: (and {<form> ...})

This function evaluates each form, one at a time,
from left to right, until one form evaluates to NIL.
In this case NIL will be returned, otherwise the
value of the last form.

example:       >(and 3 4)
               4
               >(and nil t)
               NIL
